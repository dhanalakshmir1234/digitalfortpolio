VEG

Which is the famous food of Tamil Nadu? Several varieties like saada dosai, kal dosai, muttai dosai, neer dosai, rava dosai and paasi paruppu dosai are prominently available in Tamil Nadu. Vadai is based on the ingredients used and served along with idlis. Pongal is a traditional cuisine where rice is cooked in a matki with water and milk.

NON-VEG
